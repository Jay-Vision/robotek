About US

“Jay Robotek” is an innovative IT training company that is poised to empower pupils and students with essential technological and digital skills, foster creativity and curiosity, and prepare them for the digital age. Our programme covers the training (hands on) of pupils from Nursery 2 to Primary 6 and Junior Secondary (JS) 1 to Senior Secondary (SS) 3 in primary and secondary schools respectively.

OUR GOAL

Empowering Digital and Technological Explorers: Igniting curiosity and confidence in young minds to explore the digital world.

VISION

Our vision is to create a generation of confident, tech-savvy learners who can leverage technology to solve real-world problems. We believe that every child has the potential to be a digital and technological wiz!

OUR TEAM

Our teaching faculty consists of experiences and committed professionals
who are poised to nurture young minds by providing comprehensive and engaging technology education.

Services

Teaching Approach

Hands-On Learning: We believe in learning by doing. Our classes are interactive, project-based, and encourage experimentation.
Creativity and Problem-Solving: Students explore real-world challenges and develop creative solutions using technology.
Collaboration: Teamwork and collaboration are integral to our programs.

ASSESSMENT AND EVALUATION

Regular assessments to gauge student progress Practical projects, quizzes, and coding challenges Parent-teacher conferences to discuss individual growth Inter, intra, national and international competition for exposure.

COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT

Workshops for parents and teachers
Tech fairs and exhibitions
Partnerships with local schools and organizations
